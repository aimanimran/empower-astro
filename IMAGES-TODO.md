# Images to add

Copy your existing image files into `public/assets/images/`. The site
references these exact filenames:

- [ ] `logo.png` — small nav/header logo
- [ ] `full_logo.png` — large logo (home hero)
- [ ] `full_logo_white.png` — white logo (footer)
- [ ] `favicon.svg` — browser tab icon
- [ ] `doctor.png` — Dr. Mushtaq photo (About, Blog)
- [ ] `couch.png` — office waiting room (Contact)
- [ ] `kids.png` — patients photo (Feedback)
- [ ] `conditions.png` — conditions section (Services)

Just drop them in with these names and everything wires up automatically.
They were in your old `assets/images/` folder, so copy that whole folder's
contents into `public/assets/images/`.

> The site will build and run without these, but images will appear broken
> until they're added.
