This is a sample post so you can see how the blog works. Replace it with your
own writing, or delete it once you've published a real post.

## Writing posts

Each post is a single file. You can write in **bold**, *italics*, lists, links,
and headings — all of which render automatically:

- Add a new post from the CMS ("New Blog Post"), or
- Create a new `.md` file in `src/content/blog/`.

## What controls the post

The block at the very top (between the `---` lines) is the post's settings:
its title, description, date, and author. Everything below it is the post body.

When you publish, the post appears automatically on the Blog page, newest
first, and gets its own page.
